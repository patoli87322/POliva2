So this is Me

Bonjour, My name is Patricia Oliva. I am a sofware engineer and psychology major. I am also minoring in mathematics, because i hate myself but am good at math. I am honestly just trying to survive, and not die off instantly as i am essentially two chicken nuggets stacked on top of each other hiding under a trench coat trying to resemble a person.

When i have a free moment I enjoy watching Anime, reading, painting and playing volleyball. While of course listening to the most depressing music known to existance. Why you may ask, it is soothing and suprisingly makes me happy. I am dying.

I can be an extremely eccentric person but i mean well. I am usually a mess and not even a hot one at that. My closests friends have a conspiracy theory that I was secretly a dog in a previous life, and I am not even going to deny it, because I whole heartedly agree.
